The Vision

Melghat Vikas Foundation envisions a transformative pilgrimage destination that celebrates Adivasi heritage across India—from Madhya Pradesh to Maharashtra, Chhattisgarh, and beyond.

By harnessing three sacred rivers, pristine forests, and majestic hills, we create sustainable economic opportunities while preserving tribal culture and empowering indigenous communities.

Landmark Projects

Transformative infrastructure and cultural centers

🏆 PRIMARY LANDMARK

Sant Jatu Baba Museum & Temple Complex

A grand architectural complex celebrating tribal freedom fighters and cultural heritage. Features galleries, interactive exhibits, and spiritual spaces that attract pilgrims from across India.

  • ✓ Tribal Heritage & Freedom Gallery
  • ✓ Interactive Cultural Exhibits
  • ✓ Capacity: 5,000+ visitors daily
✨ SPIRITUAL SYMBOL

100-Foot Birsa Munda Statue

A towering tribute to Birsa Munda, the legendary Adivasi freedom fighter. This iconic monument overlooks the sacred valley and serves as a beacon of tribal pride and resilience.

  • ✓ Architectural marvel with museum base
  • ✓ Panoramic valley views
  • ✓ Sacred gathering space
🏗️ MASTER CAMPUS

Integrated Pilgrimage & Tourism Campus

A comprehensive development spanning across Melghat's three rivers. Features spiritual centers, cultural zones, eco-lodges, adventure activities, and vendor markets supporting local economy.

  • ✓ Hotels & Heritage Homestays
  • ✓ Boat operators & River activities
  • ✓ Local vendor markets & restaurants
