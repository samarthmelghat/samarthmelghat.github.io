The Vision

Melghat has been
neglected for decades.
That changes now.

For generations, the tribal communities of Melghat have lived on the margins — despite sitting on one of Maharashtra's most extraordinary ecological and cultural treasures. Samarth Melghat is not a tourism project. It is a comprehensive transformation blueprint.

We envision a Melghat where every tribal family has dignified livelihood, where ancient heritage fuels modern economy, where conservation and development are not enemies but allies.

"Melghat will not remain a footnote in development reports. It will become the gold standard for how India empowers its tribal heartlands."

Six Pillars of Transformation

Pillar 1
Infrastructure & Connectivity

Roads That Connect

280 km all-weather roads, bridges, mobile towers in every pocket, broadband to every panchayat.

₹680 Cr
Pillar 2
Tribal-Owned Eco-Tourism

Tourism That Serves Locals

Tribal-run homestays, guided experiences, craft markets — 80% revenue stays in the community.

₹450 Cr

Forest-Based Economy

From Forest to Fortune

Mahua, honey, bamboo, tendu — processed, branded, sold nationally.

₹320 Cr

Digital Melghat

Bridging the Digital Divide

e-governance kiosks, digital literacy for 15,000 youth, telemedicine, online marketplace.
